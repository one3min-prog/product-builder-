The Ancient Art of Astrology

Astrology has been practiced for over 4,000 years across civilizations including Babylon, Egypt, Greece, and China. The zodiac system we use today originated from Hellenistic astrology, which mapped the positions of celestial bodies to human affairs and natural phenomena.

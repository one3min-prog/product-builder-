Complete MBTI Personality Type Guide

Explore all 16 Myers-Briggs personality types with detailed characteristics, strengths, weaknesses, and relationship insights.

The Analysts (NT Types)

Strategic thinkers who value logic, innovation, and competence. They approach life with intellectual curiosity and systematic planning.

INTJ - The Architect

Introverted, Intuitive, Thinking, Judging

Strategic masterminds with strong vision and determination. INTJs are independent thinkers who combine innovation with careful planning. In relationships, they seek intellectual equals who respect their autonomy.

Strengths: Strategic, determined, independent, insightful

Best matches: ENFP, ENTP

INTP - The Logician

Introverted, Intuitive, Thinking, Perceiving

Innovative philosophers who thrive on theoretical exploration. INTPs are curious and analytical, often absorbed in complex problems. They value authenticity and intellectual connection in relationships.

Strengths: Analytical, objective, creative, open-minded

Best matches: ENTJ, ENFJ

ENTJ - The Commander

Extraverted, Intuitive, Thinking, Judging

Bold leaders who drive toward their goals with confidence. ENTJs are decisive and ambitious, naturally taking charge in any situation. They seek partners who can match their intensity and ambition.

Strengths: Confident, strategic, charismatic, efficient

Best matches: INTP, INFP

ENTP - The Debater

Extraverted, Intuitive, Thinking, Perceiving

Clever innovators who love intellectual challenges. ENTPs are quick-witted and enjoy exploring new ideas from every angle. They keep relationships exciting with their playful, spontaneous nature.

Strengths: Creative, quick-thinking, charismatic, resourceful

Best matches: INFJ, INTJ

The Diplomats (NF Types)

Empathetic idealists who are guided by their values and seek meaningful connections. They're natural counselors and advocates for positive change.

INFJ - The Advocate

Introverted, Intuitive, Feeling, Judging

The rarest type, INFJs combine deep insight with principled idealism. They're private but deeply caring, often working behind the scenes to help others. They seek soulmate connections and meaningful depth.

Strengths: Insightful, principled, compassionate, determined

Best matches: ENFP, ENTP

INFP - The Mediator

Introverted, Intuitive, Feeling, Perceiving

Idealistic dreamers with rich inner worlds and strong values. INFPs are creative and empathetic, seeking authenticity in all aspects of life. They're devoted partners who remember every meaningful detail.

Strengths: Empathetic, creative, open-minded, passionate

Best matches: ENFJ, ENTJ

ENFJ - The Protagonist

Extraverted, Intuitive, Feeling, Judging

Charismatic leaders who inspire others through warmth and conviction. ENFJs are natural nurturers who genuinely care about others' well-being. They're devoted partners who may put others' needs above their own.

Strengths: Charismatic, empathetic, reliable, natural leader

Best matches: INFP, INTP

ENFP - The Campaigner

Extraverted, Intuitive, Feeling, Perceiving

Enthusiastic free spirits who find joy in connection and possibilities. ENFPs are curious about people and ideas, bringing energy and creativity to everything. They're passionate partners who make others feel special.

Strengths: Enthusiastic, creative, sociable, free-spirited

Best matches: INFJ, INTJ

The Sentinels (SJ Types)

Practical and reliable individuals who value tradition, stability, and service to others. They're the backbone of communities and organizations.

ISTJ - The Logistician

Introverted, Sensing, Thinking, Judging

Responsible and thorough individuals who honor their commitments. ISTJs are practical and dependable, valuing tradition and clear expectations. They show love through consistent actions and reliability.

Strengths: Honest, responsible, calm, practical

Best matches: ESFP, ESTP

ISFJ - The Defender

Introverted, Sensing, Feeling, Judging

Warm protectors who care deeply for those around them. ISFJs are loyal and observant, remembering details about the people they love. They express love through acts of service and creating comfortable environments.

Strengths: Supportive, reliable, observant, patient

Best matches: ESTP, ESFP

ESTJ - The Executive

Extraverted, Sensing, Thinking, Judging

Organized administrators who value order and responsibility. ESTJs are direct and confident, taking charge to ensure things run smoothly. They're devoted partners who create stable, structured environments.

Strengths: Organized, dedicated, direct, honest

Best matches: ISFP, ISTP

ESFJ - The Consul

Extraverted, Sensing, Feeling, Judging

Caring and sociable individuals who thrive on helping others. ESFJs are warm and attentive, creating harmony in their communities. They're nurturing partners who value family traditions and social connections.

Strengths: Caring, sociable, loyal, practical

Best matches: ISTP, ISFP

The Explorers (SP Types)

Spontaneous and practical individuals who live in the moment. They're adaptable and skilled at responding to immediate situations.

ISTP - The Virtuoso

Introverted, Sensing, Thinking, Perceiving

Bold experimenters who love to understand how things work. ISTPs are practical and observant, excellent at problem-solving under pressure. They show love through actions rather than words.

Strengths: Practical, observant, analytical, adaptable

Best matches: ESFJ, ESTJ

ISFP - The Adventurer

Introverted, Sensing, Feeling, Perceiving

Gentle artists who experience life through their senses and emotions. ISFPs are creative and sensitive, appreciating beauty in all forms. They're caring partners who express love through thoughtful gestures.

Strengths: Artistic, sensitive, charming, imaginative

Best matches: ESTJ, ESFJ

ESTP - The Entrepreneur

Extraverted, Sensing, Thinking, Perceiving

Energetic thrill-seekers who dive into action. ESTPs are observant and pragmatic, quickly responding to immediate challenges. They keep relationships exciting with their spontaneous, adventurous nature.

Strengths: Energetic, perceptive, direct, sociable

Best matches: ISFJ, ISTJ

ESFP - The Entertainer

Extraverted, Sensing, Feeling, Perceiving

Spontaneous entertainers who bring joy to every situation. ESFPs are warm and generous, loving to be around people and create fun experiences. They're affectionate partners who live in the moment.

Strengths: Bold, original, practical, observant

Best matches: ISTJ, ISFJ
